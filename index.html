With the support of Prague Wine Trophy, Prague Spirits Trophy rewards spirits from across the globe.

After 16 years of earning both local and international trust, the contest Prague Wine Trophy — PWT rapidly grew in importance. We are stepping up to the spirits category and taking further advantage of our complex knowledge of evaluating alcoholic beverages. Prague Spirits Trophy has been established as an independent contest aiming to deliver feedback on the quality of spirits of both local and global importance.

Brandies, Fruit spirits, Bitters, Whiskies, Rums, Vodkas, Gins, among others, are judged and evaluated by a jury of international professionals.
