How to Apply

Simply fill in the application form, send it to admin@praguespiritstrophy.cz and ship us two botles of each sample.

Tasting dates and categories

The first round will take place on 15. — 16. 2. 2023 in Prague. You can submit samples in the following categories:

  • Brandy
  • Pomace Brandy
  • Fruit Spirits
  • Whisky
  • Rum
  • Vodka
  • Gin & Genever
  • Bitter
  • Cocktails
  • Liqueur
  • Crème de Fruits and Cream
  • Anise-Flavoured
  • Aquavit
  • Mexican Spirits
  • Non-alcoholic spirits

    • Judging committee

    Professionals, spirits importers and producers, educated sommeliers and experienced tasters.

    Method of Assessment

    Tasting samples by category, blind, 20 samples per flight, tasting each sample 3x times, discussing each sample.

    No price categories, no design awards.

    Application

    Please fill in the complete application form including billing details and your contact address. Each sample must be assigned to a category.

    Each sample must be assigned to a category.

    Number of bottles

    Two bottles per tasting sample are required.

    Sample Identification

    Prague Spirits Trophy and the name of the contender must be written on the box.

    Entrance fee

    An Entrance €89 is to be paid per each tasting sample.

    Delivery Address

    Please submit shipment of samples to:

    U Háje 1700, 252 63 Roztoky, Czechia
